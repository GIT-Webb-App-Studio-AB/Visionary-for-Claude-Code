## Becoming a contributor

PRs welcome on the [public repository](https://github.com/GIT-Webb-App-Studio-AB/Visionary-for-Claude-Code).
Please read [`docs/superpowers/specs/`](docs/superpowers/specs/) for current
design docs before opening substantive feature PRs.

Bug reports and design feedback: open an issue with a minimal reproduction
or a screenshot showing the failure mode (the v1.5.0 gate itself was born
from a single user-supplied screenshot pair — that level of evidence is
exactly what we want).
