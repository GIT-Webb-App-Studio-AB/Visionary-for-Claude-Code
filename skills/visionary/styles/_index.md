## Transplantation Pairs (cross-domain application)
- **Newspaper Grid → Logistics** — Broadsheet authority applied where no one expects editorial rigour
- **Swiss Grid → Healthcare** — Precision + information hierarchy creates trust in clinical contexts
- **Bauhaus → SaaS** — Form-follows-function applied to software UI signals structured confidence

## Blocked Pairs (never transplant)
- **Psychedelic × Healthcare** — Visual overload in clinical contexts causes anxiety and trust failure
- **Anxious Energy × Finance** — Urgency design triggers panic selling and destroys user agency
- **Playful × Legal/GovTech** — Whimsy signals incompetence in high-stakes institutional contexts
